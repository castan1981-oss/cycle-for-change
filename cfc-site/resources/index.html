LGBTQ mental health resources

A short, honest list of where to turn — crisis lines you can reach right now, peer support staffed by queer and trans people, and directories for affirming therapy. Sorted by life stage, because what helps at 15 isn't always what helps at 45.

If you're struggling right now: the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline is free and 24/7 — call or text 988. For LGBTQ youth, the Trevor Project is at 1-866-488-7386 (or text START to 678-678).
