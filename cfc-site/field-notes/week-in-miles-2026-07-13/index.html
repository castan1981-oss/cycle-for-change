Week in Miles: Jul 7–13

Five rides, 279.6 miles, 8,028 feet of climbing — a week that started at 111°F and ended with back-to-back centuries when the overcast finally broke the heat.

Total 279.6 17h 39m — 8,028 ft —

The week split at the weather line. Tuesday and Wednesday were full Phoenix summer — clear sky, 111°F, no shade, no mercy. Both rides started before 6 a.m. and you still felt the heat pressing in by mile ten. Shorter mileage was the right call. You don't push a century into that.

Thursday was off the bike. Friday evening, six miles just to keep the legs moving — overcast had rolled in and the temperature had dropped to 77. Then Saturday morning: 75°F, clouds holding. I put down 103 miles. Sunday, 97 more. That's the deal with Phoenix in July. You wait for the break, then you use every hour of it.

279.6 miles this week. 8,028 feet of climbing. All of it goes on the board toward 7,500. The pledge exists because the communities we're riding for — queer communities — deserve better-funded mental health resources, not better-marketed ones.
