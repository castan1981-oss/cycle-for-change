The story

The miles are the message.

Cycle for Change is one person, one year, three disciplines — and a community that shows up. 7,500 miles isn't a stunt. It's a sustained act of visibility for queer communities, routed through the orgs doing the work on the ground.

Phoenix to everywhere. Cold dawn rides, long pool sets, desert heat. The board grows with every name. The vote decides where the giving lands. Endurance as activism — not a slogan, a calendar.
