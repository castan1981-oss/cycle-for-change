The story

Queer in the field.

Photo

I'm queer in the field — a long way back from a sober-living bed in Prescott, riding miles for the communities that kept people like me alive.

Cycle for Change is one person, one year, three disciplines. 7,500 miles isn't a stunt. It's visibility — routed through orgs doing the work on the ground.

You pledge a mile. I do the miles. We vote. You give direct. That's the whole machine.
