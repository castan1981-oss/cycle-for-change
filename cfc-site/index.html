how it works

From your name to the finish line.

Four steps. No middleman, no overhead cut, no card today.

01
Pledge a mile. Add your name. You're on the board — no amount, no card.
02
I do the miles. 7,500 across swim, bike and run. Tracked live, fed by Strava.
03
We vote. When the year closes, pledgers pick which queer-community orgs get it.
04
You give direct. Straight to the orgs we chose. Where you voted is where it lands.
